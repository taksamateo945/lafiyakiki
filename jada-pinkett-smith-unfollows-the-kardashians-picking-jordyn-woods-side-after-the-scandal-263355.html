Jada Pinkett Smith Unfollows The Kardashians Picking Jordyn Woods Side After The Scandal?

August 25, 2024
blog

If there was any doubt before, people are now certain Jada Pinkett Smith has picked a side in the Jordyn Woods vs. Kardashians drama that started after the model’s alleged kiss with Khloe’s baby daddy, Tristan. That being said, the actress showed her allegiance towards Jordyn in a very public way – by simply unfollowing some KarJenners on social media!

That being said, while momager Kris Jenner, as well as Khloe and Kim, are still following Jada on Instagram at this time, the Red Table Talk host has hit the ‘unfollow button’ for the trio.

About a month ago, Jada gave Jordyn the opportunity to tell her side of the story on her Facebook talk show when the scandal exploded.

She pretty much proved then that she was on Jordyn’s team, but she is still doing it today by unfollowing Khloe, Kim, and Kris.

On Red Table Talk, Jordyn claimed Tristan unexpectedly kissed her as she was leaving his house party, defending herself against previous reports that seemingly exaggerated their interaction.

Rumors were going around that they went all the way and that even before the hookup, Jordyn gave Tristan a lap dance at the bash.

Jordyn told Jada at the time that ‘This story that didn’t have to be what it was turned into the biggest scandal or betrayal of the year…They’re putting all their focus on a young black woman who just made a mistake. And not a mistake that is worth public crucifixion. It is a mistake that should’ve been dealt with internally.’
