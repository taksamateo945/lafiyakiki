Singer Geneva Cruz is currently on vacation in Balesin Island. Cruz spent some time at the beach, and shared some photos from the day on Instagram. In them, she posed in a tree and on the sand. Cruz shared some of her wellness secrets in an interview with Philstar.com. She says that she decided to cut alcohol out. "I stopped drinking alcohol three years ago. It seems that I have already graduated there, I have already settled there. I know for a fact that alcohol can make your skin dry, and when the skin is dry, it is easier to get wrinkles."

Cruz opened up about her diet to Philstar.com. She says that while she eats healthy, she also believes in balance. "I love vegetables, I love protein. I eat rice but not so much. I eat cakes. Sometimes five cup cakes, you know that? I'm just being a woman who loves sweets. But as much as possible, every day, I watch what I eat."

Cruz is all about being happy. She shared some of her secrets for doing this to Philstar.com. "I choose to be happy," Cruz says. "I choose to look at the bright side. Every day, so I don't feel sad, I just think of the things I have now. I focus of what I have instead of things I don't have."
