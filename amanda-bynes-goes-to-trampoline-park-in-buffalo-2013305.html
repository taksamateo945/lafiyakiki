Amanda Bynes Trampoline Park Brown Wig Picture: Star Has &quot;Great Time&quot;

July 14, 2024
blog

Amanda Bynes is apparently hopped up on . . . trampolines. On Wednesday, the 27-year-old former child star apparently ditched NYC, where she’s been making one bizarre headline after another, to head upstate. Her ultimate destination? A Sky Zone indoor trampoline park in Buffalo, New York.

“Amanda had a great time here,” Rhonda See, Sky Zone Buffalo owner tells Us Weekly, adding that Bynes used her middle name, Laura, to sign up. “She came to check out a workout class and then stayed to jump on the trampolines.”

While Bynes’ reported appearance in Buffalo remains unexplained — there’s speculation of a nearby movie shoot — See told Us that the troubled actress’ appearance at Sky Zone was a good thing. “For us, this is a positive, healthy place. For someone who is going through a rough time like her, it was nice to see her having a great time. She came to burn some calories and have some fun.”

Added See: “She seemed mentally fine.”
