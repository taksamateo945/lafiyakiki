Questions that we need answering in Pretty Little Liars 6B

April 18, 2024
blog

It has certainly been a long 5 months since the finale of Pretty Little Liars “Game Over, Charles” that aired on August 11th, 2015; but one of our favorite TV shows is finally coming back in action THIS Tuesday!

But before we run through our questions, let’s all take a moment to remember that WE FINALLY FOUND OUT WHO “A” WAS!! It only took 6 seasons of constant stress and anxiety but we made it and we’re still (barely) alive! #CeCeisA

Okay now we can move on…

Question 1: Why is Alison teaching at Rosewood High School? 

It may not be that important of a question, but you would assume that after all this time, none of the liars would have any desire to stay in Rosewood… ESPECIALLY Alison. There must be a reason she’s still around.

Question 2: What happened to Haleb?

It has been confirmed that Hanna is engaged, but her lucky fiancé has not been exposed. In the trailers that we’ve seen however, it doesn’t look likely that Caleb is in the picture.  *sigh*

Question 3: Did Alison and the liars forgive CeCe? Where is she now? 

We’re quite curious to find out where CeCe is and what she’s been doing. After revealing herself, do you think the girls would find the courage to forgive her? I know I certainly wouldn’t be able to.

Question 4: WHERE IS EMILY?

Yes, she’s made appearances in the trailers but there’s no way it’s enough to satisfy our needs. From the looks of things, she seems to be in some kind of hospital. Perhaps… Radley?

Question 5: Are Aria and Ezra still together?

We’ve been given bits of Ezria in the sneak peaks but probably shouldn’t get our hopes up and label them as a couple. What we can predict, is that Aria has landed a job in the field of journalism; as she sits so beautifully at her desk.

Question 6: Did Spencer get bangs and make a change because Spoby are no longer?

Well, I definitely hope not! It’s a known fact that Spencer and Toby truly loved each other, but sometimes when people experience tough breakups, they result in drastic, impulsive changes. Regardless of what she went through, it’s safe to say she’s remained absolutely stunning. 

Question 7: Why did Melissa move back to Rosewood? 

Life would be a lot less complex if Melissa just stayed in London, but obviously there is more to be revealed about her past. After all, she was the one who killed Bethany Young– why is she not in prison for that?

Question 8: Will Jason ever find out the truth about CeCe?

I can’t possibly see that going over well.

And the final question… Will the girls EVER STOP LYING? 

It’s no secret that Marlene King has a lot of loose ends to tie within this upcoming season, but will all of our questions be answered? I’m going with probably not.
