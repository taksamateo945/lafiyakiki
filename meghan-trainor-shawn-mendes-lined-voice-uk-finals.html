Meghan Trainor, Shawn Mendes lined up for Voice UK finals

March 03, 2024
blog

BBC have announced the guest performers for this years live shows.

Meghan Trainor, Years & Years, Birdy, Zara Larsson, Shawn Mendes and Stevie McCrorie are all set to perform during the finals, which will be the last shows on BBC before the talent show moves to ITV in 2017.

Check out the line-up of guest performers below.

March 26 – Quarter Final

  • Stevie McCrorie
  • Years and Years

April 2 – Semi Final

  • Birdy
  • Zara Larsson

April 9 – Final

  • Meghan Trainor
  • Shawn Mendes

The Voice UK goes live this Saturday at 7pm on BBC One.
