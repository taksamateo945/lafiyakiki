About

Rihanna is a Barbadian singer, actress, and businesswoman. She was born on February 20, 1988, in Saint Michael, Barbados. Rihanna first gained fame with her debut single “Pon de Replay” in 2005, and since then, she has released several successful albums and singles, including “Umbrella,” “Disturbia,” “Only Girl (In the World),” and “Work.”

Apart from her music career, Rihanna has also acted in several films, including “Battleship,” “Ocean’s 8,” and “Guava Island.” She has also ventured into business and launched her fashion line, Fenty, which includes cosmetics, lingerie, and luxury clothing. Rihanna is known for her philanthropic work and activism, particularly in the areas of education and healthcare in developing countries.
