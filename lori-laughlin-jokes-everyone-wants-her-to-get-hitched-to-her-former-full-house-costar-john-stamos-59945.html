Lori Loughlin Says Everyone Wishes She Had Married Her Former Full House Costar John Stamos

July 08, 2024
blog

Uncle Jesse and Aunt Becky forever!

Despite her fond memories of costarring with John Stamos in Full House, Lori Loughlin admits she has been totally surprised by the number of fans who wish the onscreen couple had ended up together in real life.

John and Lori as Jesse and Becky on Full House in 1993.

“It’s caught me off guard lately. John and I had so much fun together, but even I am like, why are people so obsessed with these two?” the 50-year-old actress — who is happily married to designer Mossimo Giannulli — said in a new interview.

“I don’t post many pictures of my own husband on Instagram because every time I do, I get a bunch of comments saying, ‘You should have married John Stamos,'” she continued.

Lori and her husband, Mossimo, in 2008.

Though she said the constant fascination with her love life is strange, she noted she actually finds it somewhat “flattering.” “Maybe we represented some kind of idyllic couple,” the mom-of-two told People.

John, 51, and Lori — who starred on Full House from 1988 until 1995 — will reprise their roles as Jesse Katsopolis and Becky Donaldson Katsopolis, respectively, in the upcoming Netflix series, Fuller House.
