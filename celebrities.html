Name:Dina Manzo

Hometown:Franklin Lakes, NJ

Birthday:March 7, 1972

About

Dina Manzo is best known as a cast member on the reality series The Real Housewives of New Jersey. Like her fellow cast members, she lives in the town of Franklin Lakes, N.J. She is married to Tommy Manzo who co-owns the event and catering facility The Brownstone with his brother, Albert, who is real housewife Caroline Manzo‘s husband. She has a 14-year-old daughter, Alexia, from a previous marriage. Manzo is the founder of the non-profit organization Project Ladybug, which helps children with life threatening conditions.

Manzo is most infamous for her drama on the show with Danielle Staub. Manzo quit the Real Housewives in the middle of season two citing her drama with Staub as the reason for her departure.
