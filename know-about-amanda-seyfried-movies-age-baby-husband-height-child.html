Short Bio: Age, Height

Amanda Seyfried, age 33, was born on 3 December 1985 in Allentown, Pennsylvania as Amanda Michelle Seyfried. She is the second daughter of her pharmacist father, Jack Seyfried, and occupational therapist mother, Ann (née Sander). Amanda has an older sister named Jennifer Seyfried, who is a musician in the Philadelphia organ-driven rock band Love City.

Standing at an average height of 5 feet and 3 inches (1.6 m), Amanda completed her graduation from Allentown’s William Allen High School in 2003. Following that, she enrolled at Fordham University only to later leave for her role in Mean Girls.

Career In TV And Movies

Before her character of Karen Smith in 2004 American teen comedy flick Mean Girls, Amanda commenced her acting career some years ago in 1999. She landed series regular on soap opera As the World Turns from 1999 to 2001. Later, she garnered more TV exposure with All My Children (2003), Law & Order: Special Victims Unit (2004), and Wildfire (2006).
