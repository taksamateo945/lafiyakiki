Will There Be a Fourth Season of Sweet Magnolias? After a long, painful wait for new episodes, Sweet Magnolias season 3 finally came out on Netflix in July 2023, giving the show’s three main characters a chance to let it all out. Fans have to wonder if there will be a Sweet Magnolias season 4 after the third season was so good.

The third season starts after the end of the second season, when Maddie had to deal with Cal’s public outburst and Helen had to deal with Erik and Ryan’s relationship. While this was going on, someone from the Sullivans’ past came to stir things up.

Again, season 3 is a charming and comforting show with a lot of heart, fun, and enough drama to fill a margarita pitcher. But will the story keep going in season 4 of Sweet Magnolias?

Is Season 3 of Sweet Magnolias the Last Season?

As of July 20, when season 3 of Sweet Magnolias came out, Netflix hadn’t said if it would be the last season. But it seemed like it could be, didn’t it? Without a big cliffhanger to keep us guessing until the next season, the season finale wrapped up most of the stories in a way that could have worked as a series finale if Netflix decided to cancel the show.

All goes well at Dana Sue and Ronnie’s party to repeat their vows at the end of season 3. Maddie’s mom is off with her new possible boyfriend, Bill, and Kathy are leaving town as a couple, Helen and Erik seem ready to get back together, and Maddie and Cal are back on solid ground. The only thing that isn’t clear is what Isaac and Noreen have to say to each other.

Will There Be a Fourth Season of Sweet Magnolias?

As was said above, Netflix hasn’t decided whether to keep Sweet Magnolias going or not as of July 2023, when the third season will come out. Since the show has such devoted fans and has always been a hit with Netflix watchers, it’s unlikely that it will end after season 3. We think that Sweet Magnolias will be back for a fourth season.

Netflix usually says whether it will continue to air a show about a month after it comes out, so we should know about Sweet Magnolias by the fall, if not sooner. In the past, Netflix has taken a couple or more months to say whether or not a show would be renewed.

Fans are keeping their fingers crossed that they won’t have to wait more than a few weeks to find out what will happen to this show.

Who Could Be on Sweet Magnolias for the Fourth Season?

We couldn’t imagine a season of Sweet Magnolias without the three main characters, so we’d hope that JoAnna Garcia Swisher (Maddie Townsend), Brooke Elliot (Dana Sue Sullivan), and Heather Headley (Helen Decatur) would play their parts again.

At the time, there aren’t any clear storylines that would keep other cast members from coming back, so let’s hope that everyone comes back for season four.

One person who won’t be back is Bianca Berry Tarantino, who played Katie Townsend. Tarantino left the part before season three because of scheduling problems, and Ella Grace Helton took it over. Helton is expected to be back for season four.

Until the premiere of Sweet Magnolias season 4, there are lots of other shows to keep you occupied from the same genre. Recipe Lost And Found Season 3, Devil’s Advocate Season 2, and many more come under this category.

Conclusion
