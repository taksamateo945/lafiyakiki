Khloe Kardashian slammed as 'delusional' for believing Tristan Thompson will 'step up' &amp; be a good d

January 25, 2024
blog

FANS have branded Khloe Kardashian ‘delusional’ for believing Tristan Thompson will step up and be a good father to their baby boy.

Khloe, 38, confirmed that she and Tristan, 31, are expecting their second child, a baby boy, via surrogate.

A Kardashian fan reposted an Us Weekly article in which a source stated that Khloe has “Zero Doubt” Tristan Thompson will “Step Up” after their second child arrives.

The full insider quote claims: “One positive [Khloe has] clung onto all along is that Tristan is a wonderful dad to True”.

The source continues: “Khloe has zero doubt he’ll step up and do the right thing with their new baby too.”

The Redditor on the other hand had their doubts, captioning the post “Why do I have a hard time believing he will be a good/present dad to this new baby. What is Khloe’s deal?”

In the post’s comment thread, Kardashian fans had a lot to say about Khloe’s conception that the NBA star is presently a good dad now or that he will be when their second baby arrives.

One person wrote: “I have a hard time believing this.”

A second Redditor commented: “You can’t be a good dad if you’re only there for one of your kids.”

Another commenter wrote: “This is such a bulls**t narrative. He’s not a good dad. He hasn’t even seen one of his kids. He MIGHT possibly be a decent dad to True when you use his other [children’s] dynamics as a comparison.”

Fans are referring to the report that Tristan hasn’t, nor does he have immediate plans to, meet his son, Theo, of six months.

One fan wrote: “Imagine being an adult woman who is totally fine with a deadbeat dad showing up for your kids while shunning the rest of the children he’s fathered in the meantime.”

A second contributor added: “This idea that he can selectively be a good dad to one of his kids, but a terrible one to others is complete bulls**t.”

Another person reasoned: “If he does [step up] great. But that’s even more of a f**king f**ked up thing given he doesn’t see his other child. What a disgrace. Poor Theo.”

GOOD DAD?

One Redditor questioned: “I’d like Khloe to define what being a good dad is.”

Others responded, with one fan writing: “[Someone who] shows up for the occasional birthday party or photoshoot and doesn’t leak or expose family secrets.”

Another person replied: “[It’s] definitely not treating the mother of his children with respect.”

A third commenter simply wrote: “Khlow standards.”

“This is why he goes out of this way to show off how much he doesn’t give a s**t, partying and messing around with random chicks”, commented another.

A, very blunt, fan added: “He’s f**king all around Mykonos with literally zero discretion. He has absolutely nothing to worry about with his doormat back home.”

One person explained what a good father is, commenting: “He sees his daughter sporadically. He is not a good father. A good father cares for his children every day, is honest, responsible, and consistently available.”

An angry Redditor threw shade toward Khloe for feeling the way she does, writing: “It’s the fact that she said ‘he’s a wonderful dad to True’ she specified which child. [It] makes me sick.”

Finally, a fan commented: “He’s not a good dad and she looks dumb saying it.”

BREAKING THE SILENCE

Earlier this week, Khloe broke her silence for the first time since the announcement of her second child with ex Tristan Thompson.

The Chicago Bull’s power forward has been partying it up in Greece lately and was spotted holding hands with a mystery woman.

The Hulu star took to Instagram to post for the first time in days as she shared various photos with her only child True, four.

In the snaps, the 38-year-old rocks a camouflage swimsuit while waist-deep in beautiful clear water.

Khloe’s little one True has her arms wrapped around her famous mom as the duo strike up different poses.

The reality star captioned the batch of pics: “Me and my best girl making the best memories. I will forever have your back my angel girl.”

Fans were quick to react to the new post as one wrote: “Love you both so much,” while another added, “She really is very blessed to have you as her mama.”

“It’s great she is going to have a sibling…” one chimed in.

However, others were harsher and reacted: “Please let this be the last time! You deserve better for and your kids.”

THE ANNOUNCEMENT

The Keeping Up With the Kardashians alum shares True with her ex Tristan, but Khloe’s representative recently confirmed that the TV star is having a second child via a surrogate mother.

“Khloe is incredibly grateful to the extraordinary surrogate for such a beautiful blessing,” the rep shared.

The baby is expected to be a boy, as an insider shared with PageSix: “Khloe has always wanted a little brother for True and decided to go ahead with having the baby with the support of her family.”

While Khloe seems to be attempting to stay on a positive route, fans have noticed that the TV personality’s inner circle – including her sister Kourtney, Travis Barker, and her mom Kris Jenner – appear to have very recently stopped following Tristan on Instagram.

The famous athlete has also not helped his cause as he has continued to enjoy his getaway in Mykonos.

The 31-year-old was previously at the island’s Principote Beach Club and dined at the popular steakhouse Nusr-Et where the famous restauranteur, often referred to as Salt Bae, prepared a cut of steak at Tristan’s table.
